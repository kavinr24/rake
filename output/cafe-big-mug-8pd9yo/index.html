Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cafe Big Mug has been a beloved gathering place for coffee enthusiasts in Santa Clara. We believe that every cup tells a story — from the careful selection of ethically sourced beans to the precise art of roasting and brewing.

Our baristas are passionate artisans who treat each drink as a masterpiece. Whether you're seeking a bold espresso, a creamy latte, or a delicate pour-over, we craft it with the same dedication to excellence.
