Our Story

Founded with a passion for exceptional coffee, Caffino has been a beloved gathering place in Mountain View since day one. We source the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world, roasting them to perfection to bring out their unique character.

Every beverage is crafted with precision and care by our skilled baristas, and our pastry case features fresh-baked goods from local artisans. Whether you're here for your morning ritual or an afternoon escape, Caffino welcomes you with warmth and flavor.
