Our Story

Founded with a passion for exceptional coffee, Café LaTTea has been serving the Cupertino community since 2019. We believe in the art of slow coffee — carefully sourcing beans from sustainable farms around the world, roasting them to perfection, and crafting each drink with intention and care.

More than just a coffee shop, we're a gathering place for neighbors, a workspace for remote professionals, and a sanctuary for those seeking a moment of peace in their busy day.
