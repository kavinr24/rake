Our Story

Welcome to Milyar Coffee, where passion meets precision. Founded in the heart of Santa Clara, we believe that exceptional coffee is more than a beverage — it's an experience, a moment of connection, a daily ritual that elevates the ordinary into the extraordinary.

Our master roasters source the finest beans from sustainable farms around the world, carefully roasting each batch to unlock its unique character. Every cup we serve is crafted with intention, brewed with care, and served with warmth.
